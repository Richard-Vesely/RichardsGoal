My goal is to help a billion people live with more excitement, peace, and/or confidence.

Not in a big dramatic way — just enough that they feel it was worth taking a moment to come back here and click:

"Yes, you helped me."

How I hope to do that:

  • Inspiring courage — by nudging people to try something they didn't think they could.
  • Sparking curiosity — through questions, projects, or ideas that make the world feel more fascinating.
  • Building confidence — by giving people chances to surprise themselves with what they're capable of.
  • Encouraging self-acceptance — by helping people make peace with parts of themselves they've been fighting.
  • Challenging harmful beliefs — especially those that quietly ruin lives from the inside.
  • Sharing mistakes — so people feel less alone in theirs.
  • Creating tools and spaces — where people can grow, connect, and build something meaningful.
  • Showing what's possible — by living it myself.
